The dental care you deserve. This is a phrase that many people never think to ask for when they visit the dentist. They simply go in and get their teeth cleaned, then leave without much thought about it. But this doesn’t have to be your experience! Dental care should be taken seriously and we want what’s best for our patients – which means giving them the time and attention that they need, along with high-quality biomimetic dentistry services. As a Bloomfield Hills Biomimetic Dentist, we strive not only to give you a beautiful smile but also to help keep your mouth and original teeth healthy into the future! Buscemi Family Dentistry has been providing top-notch dental services to the community for years. They have many different treatment options, including: biomimetic dentistry, teeth whitening, implants, veneers, and much more. Dentists are known for providing services like teeth cleaning and dental checkups. But many patients come in thinking their only need is a simple teeth cleaning, when they actually have more serious problems that require attention. This could mean cavities, gingivitis (a gum disease), or even tooth decay which can lead to jaw pain, headaches and even infections of the mouth or throat.
